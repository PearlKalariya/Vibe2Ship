# Clutch — Demo Video Script (~2 min)

Read the **SAY** lines aloud. Do the **[ACTION]** on screen. Keep energy up, pace brisk.
Before recording: open the deployed app, **Reset** to a clean state, mic + notification permission ready, one screenshot of a syllabus/email with dates saved to desktop.

---

### 0:00 – 0:12 · Hook
**[ACTION]** App open on the empty state.
**SAY:** "We all miss deadlines — not because we forget, but because reminders don't actually *do* anything. Meet Clutch: an AI agent that doesn't just remind you, it plans and finishes the work for you."

### 0:12 – 0:40 · The agent loop (Agentic Depth)
**[ACTION]** Click the first sample chip *("job interview Thursday, 20-page report Friday, rent tomorrow")*. Let the tool chips fire.
**SAY:** "I hand it my chaos in plain English. Watch — Clutch isn't chatting, it's acting. It breaks the goal into tasks, ranks them by deadline risk, schedules focus blocks on my calendar, and drafts the rent email I need to send — multiple autonomous steps in one go."
**[ACTION]** Point at the Plan panel filling: Tasks → Calendar → Drafts. Hover a draft, click **Copy**.
**SAY:** "Every action is real — here's the actual email, ready to send."

### 0:40 – 1:05 · Screenshot to plan (Innovation)
**[ACTION]** Click the paperclip, attach the syllabus/email screenshot.
**SAY:** "But typing is slow. I can just paste a screenshot of my syllabus. Clutch reads the image with Gemini vision, pulls out every deadline, and turns it into a scheduled plan — instantly."
**[ACTION]** Tasks/calendar populate from the image.
**SAY:** "No manual entry. From a picture to a plan."

### 1:05 – 1:30 · Voice + live reminders
**[ACTION]** Click the mic, say *"add: call the dentist tomorrow at 5pm."* Send.
**SAY:** "It's voice-enabled too — fully hands-free."
**[ACTION]** Click **Enable reminders**. The center alert modal pops.
**SAY:** "And these reminders actually reach you — Clutch alerts you a day and thirty minutes before every deadline, so nothing slips through."

### 1:30 – 1:55 · Google tech + why it matters
**[ACTION]** Slow pan over the full UI / stats bar.
**SAY:** "Clutch is built entirely on Google's stack — built and deployed in Google AI Studio, powered by Gemini 2.5 function calling, Gemini vision, and Google Search grounding for live research. Six Google technologies working as one agent."

### 1:55 – 2:05 · Close
**[ACTION]** Land on the clean dashboard.
**SAY:** "Clutch — never miss again. Your agent handles the last mile."

---

## Recording tips
- Total: keep under 2:10. Cut dead air between actions in editing.
- If the API is busy mid-demo, the status shows "Scheduling…" — just keep talking; it recovers. Or pre-run once so it's warm.
- Record at 1280×720+ , show the whole window (both panels visible).
- One clean take of each section beats one perfect long take — you can stitch.
- End screen: show the deployed URL on screen for 2 seconds.
